KEW.AH: Live Asset (Livestock) Management System

KEW.AH contains 8 specialized forms for managing live animals including registration, health tracking, vaccination schedules, and breeding records for cattle, goats, sheep, and other livestock.

KEW.AH-1: Animal Registration

Individual animal ID assignment, species identification, physical characteristics, and initial registration. Captures breed, gender, DOB, and distinguishing features.

Open Form

KEW.AH-2: Animal Health Record

Comprehensive health tracking including treatment history, diagnoses, medications, health status monitoring, and daily observations. Maintains complete medical history.

Open Form

KEW.AH-3 & 4: Vaccination & Breeding

KEW.AH-3 tracks vaccination schedules, vaccine types, batch numbers, and next due dates. KEW.AH-4 records breeding events, mating dates, pregnancy monitoring, and offspring information.

Open Form

KEW.AH-5: Breeding Management

Comprehensive breeding records including sire and dam information, mating events, pregnancy monitoring, birth records, and offspring details. Tracks genealogy and breeding history.

Open Form

KEW.AH-6: Mortality Records

Documentation of livestock deaths including cause of death, clinical history, necropsy findings (if performed), and proper carcass disposal methods. Important for disease tracking and record keeping.

Open Form

KEW.AH-7: Livestock Transfer

Records for inter-facility animal movements, sales, loans, or donations. Captures health status at transfer, facility information, transport details, and receiving confirmation. Maintains chain of custody.

Open Form

KEW.AH-8: Daily Care Log

Daily health monitoring and care records including feeding, water provision, behavioral observations, medical treatments, facility maintenance, and caretaker notes. Essential for continuous animal welfare tracking.

Open Form
