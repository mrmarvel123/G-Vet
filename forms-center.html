How to Use Forms Center

1. Select Module

Choose KEW.PA (Assets), KEW.PS (Store), or KEW.AH (Livestock) based on your operation.

2. Choose Form

View module index to find the specific form needed for your transaction or operation.

3. Complete & Print

Fill in all required fields, print the form, obtain signatures, and file records properly.

Note: All forms are templates. Save or print before closing. Ensure proper authorization and approvals as per departmental procedures.
