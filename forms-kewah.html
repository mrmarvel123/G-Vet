Animal Registration:

  • Assign unique animal ID/tag
  • Record physical characteristics
  • Document parentage if known
  • Take identification photos
  • Complete KEW.AH-1 form

Health Management:

  • Daily health observation
  • Record all treatments
  • Maintain vaccination schedule
  • Document any illnesses
  • Update KEW.AH-2 regularly

Breeding Records:

  • Track breeding cycles
  • Record mating dates
  • Monitor pregnancy progress
  • Document birth details
  • Update offspring records

Biosecurity: Maintain strict biosecurity protocols. Report any disease outbreaks immediately. Follow quarantine procedures for new animals.
